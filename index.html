Our Story

Happy Now Farm is owned and operated by Conner Pangia, a passionate organic farmer with extensive experience in sustainable agriculture. Since 2015, we've been dedicated to growing the highest quality produce without the use of synthetic chemicals, pesticides, or fertilizers.


We grow vegetables year-round using advanced growing techniques including high tunnels, greenhouses, and protective row covers. This allows us to provide fresh, local produce even through the winter months. Our current harvest includes lettuce, arugula, peppers, eggplant, corn, fresh herbs (basil, cilantro, dill), radishes, mustard greens, escarole, chard, beans, cucumbers, zucchini, summer squash, cabbage, and broccolini.


Now partnered with Pleasant Valley Farm in Argyle, NY, Happy Now Farm continues to grow and distribute organic vegetables with a focus on quality, sustainability, and community connection. With a Permaculture Design Certification from Punta Mona, Costa Rica, Conner brings a holistic approach to farming that works with nature.

Year-Round Growing

Thanks to our high tunnels, greenhouses, and row covers, we grow fresh vegetables throughout all seasons. While summer brings our peak harvest of warm-season crops, we continue producing cold-hardy vegetables like kale, leeks, carrots, and potatoes well into fall and winter.

Coming This Fall: Fresh harvested leeks, storage potatoes, and sweet carrots. Kale returns with the cooler weather!
